Crystals Collector Game

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below and by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number listed

Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

When the game starts, each crystals will have different values
